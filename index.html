1. Who We Are

Shift Vault is an independent mobile application developed by Jakub Tuzinowski for personal shift tracking and invoice management.

The App is not affiliated with any employer, agency, or third-party business.

Contact: shiftvaultapp@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

The App collects only data you voluntarily enter:

  • Your full name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Bank account details (sort code, account number) — used solely for generating invoices
  • UTR / VAT number (optional, used on invoices only)
  • Shift records (dates, times, locations, pay rates, notes)
  • Client details (names, addresses, contact information)
  • Invoice records and generated PDF files
During initial setup, you are asked to provide your full name. This is stored locally on your device and used to pre-fill your invoices.

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored exclusively on your device. Your data is never transmitted to any external server, cloud service, or third party by this App.

Data is stored using an on-device SQLite database. No internet connection is required to use the App.

4. Third-Party SDKs

This App is built using Expo (Expo Technology Inc.) and React Native (Meta Platforms Inc.). These frameworks may collect limited technical data such as:

  • Anonymous crash reports and error logs
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Anonymous usage diagnostics

This data is collected by Expo / Meta, not by the App developer, and is subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • Expo Privacy Policy
  • Meta Privacy Policy

The App does not use advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or behavioural analytics of any kind.

5. Device Permissions

  • Calendar access — only when using "Import from Calendar"; read-only; no data transmitted externally
  • Document / file access — only when importing backup files or exporting PDF invoices; all processing is local

6. Legal Basis for Processing (UK GDPR)

  • Article 6(1)(a) — Consent: you provide data voluntarily
  • Article 6(1)(b) — Contract performance: invoice generation for your professional work
  • Article 6(1)(f) — Legitimate interests: security and App functionality

7. Data Retention

  • All personal data is retained on your device for as long as you keep the App installed
  • Profile save timestamps are automatically purged after 14 days
  • You may delete all data at any time via Settings → Danger Zone → Delete All Data
  • Uninstalling the App permanently removes all locally stored data

8. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

  • Right of Access — view all data stored in the App at any time
  • Right to Rectification — edit or correct your data in Settings
  • Right to Erasure — delete all data via Settings → Danger Zone
  • Right to Data Portability — export your data via Settings → Data & Backup
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — uninstalling the App constitutes full withdrawal

Since all data is stored locally on your device, most rights can be exercised directly within the App.

9. Profile Update Security Limit

The App enforces a limit of 3 profile saves per 14-day rolling period to protect your personal and financial details from unauthorised modification.

The timestamps used to enforce this limit:

  • Contain only the date and time of each save action
  • Do not contain any personal information
  • Are stored solely on your device
  • Expire and are deleted automatically after 14 days

10. Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's native security features. We recommend:

  • Using a screen lock or biometric authentication on your device
  • Creating regular backups via Settings → Data & Backup
  • Not sharing your device with unauthorised persons

11. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for use by adults (18+) for professional work tracking purposes. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18 years of age.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified within the App and asked to accept any material changes before continuing to use the App.
